BIO

Jwagner

Jwagner is an Illustration major at FIT in New York City. He is in his 4th semester and applying for the Animation Program for his BFA. Jwagner likes to create psychedelic pieces and pieces that make your mind think and also simple ones that are just nice to look at. He has worked at the MOMA (Museam of Modern Art) as a temp for 6 months as a sales associate. For the summer of 2017, Jwagner is applying for an internship at Viacom, which is a networking company that owns Nickolodean, Comedy Central, MTV, and VH1.
